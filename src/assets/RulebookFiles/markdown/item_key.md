# Key

- ***Side*** - Can be used in the offhand and taken as a Side Weapon
- ***Versatile*** - One-handed normally but gain +2 dmg if Two-handed
- ***Throwable*** - Throw the item within 6 tiles of you.
- ***Reaching X*** - Melee attack at range of #
- ***Loading X*** - Can be used # of times before a dice must be used on it to reload
- ***Autoload*** - Refills all ammo at end of turn.
- ***Consumable*** - Cannot be reacquired once used.
- ***Glow*** - You may spend # to illuminate up to 2 tiles around the item. (Lasts until you dispel it.)


- ***On Hit*** - Applied if the target fails a Dodge roll.
- ***Bonus*** - Is applied when giving an 7 or less for an Attack Sum.
- ***Downside*** - Is applied when giving an 11 or higher for an Attack Sum.
